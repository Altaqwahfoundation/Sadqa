"The Prophet said, 'The upper hand is better than the lower hand...'"

Aim of the Foundation

The aim of Al Taqwah Foundation is to foster lasting change by providing aid to those in need, focusing on sustainable projects that uplift communities. Through education, healthcare, and economic empowerment, we aim to break the cycle of poverty and create a brighter future for all.

2:265 وَمَثَلُ ٱلَّذِينَ يُنفِقُونَ أَمْوَٰلَهُمُ ٱبْتِغَآءَ مَرْضَاتِ ٱللَّهِ وَتَثْبِيتًۭا مِّنْ أَنفُسِهِمْ كَمَثَلِ جَنَّةٍۭ بِرَبْوَةٍ أَصَابَهَا وَابِلٌۭ فَـَٔاتْ أُكُلَهَا ضِعْفَيْنِ فَإِن لَّمْ يُصِبْهَا وَابِلٌۭ فَطَلٌّۭ ۗ وَٱللَّهُ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ بَصِيرٌ ٢٦٥

And the example of those who donate their wealth, seeking Allah’s pleasure and believing the reward is certain,[1] is that of a garden on a fertile hill: when heavy rain falls, it yields up twice its normal produce. If no heavy rain falls, a drizzle is sufficient. And Allah is All-Seeing of what you do. — Dr. Mustafa Khattab, The Clear Quran [1] It could also mean “… seeking Allah’s pleasure and proving their sincerity.”
